About us

Dynamic Building Solutions is one of India’s leading companies in Pre-Engineered Building (PEB) manufacturing, supply, and civil construction services. With operations spread across Pan India, we deliver complete turnkey project solutions — from concept to completion.

We are committed to providing economical, high-quality, and innovative building solutions that meet the diverse needs of industries, commercial establishments, and infrastructure projects. Our strength lies in our integrated approach, combining designing, planning, manufacturing, logistics, execution, and delivery under one roof.
